Programs and Services

WTCG is here to help.

Over the last 25 years SAFE Haven for Cats has developed a wide range of programs and services to serve our community. But all of them are focused on achieving our mission: To use no-kill principles and education to save cats’ lives through rescue, adoption, spay / neuter services and community programs.

Maybe you are adopting your first cat . . .
Maybe you want to get your cat spayed but you can’t afford it . . .
Maybe you found a sweet cat near your home . . .
Maybe you just retired and want meaningful volunteer service . . .
Maybe you have been laid off and need help feeding your cat for a little while . . .
Maybe you have a feral cat in your neighborhood and don’t know what to do to help it . . .
Maybe you are a young person with a passion for cats . . .

In all of these situations SAFE Haven for Cats is here to help. Click on the sections to get more information.
